Tourism in Malaysia

Trends, destinations and performance

Tourism in Malaysia rose strongly before the pandemic, collapsed in 2020, and recovered unevenly afterward across states, hotel performance, and spending patterns.

Peak annual arrivals

26.8M

2016 recorded the highest full-year arrivals.
