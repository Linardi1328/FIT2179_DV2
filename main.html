How visitor demand changed across time

Monthly Tourist Arrivals

This chart shows the full tourism story: growth before 2020, collapse during the pandemic, and gradual recovery afterward.

Why it matters: This is the clearest chart for understanding the tourism shock.

Recovery Index by Quarter

This chart compares each quarter against the 2019 average. Values below 100 remain under the pre-pandemic benchmark.

Why it matters: It shows recovery progress more clearly than raw totals alone.

Pre-COVID Monthly Arrival Intensity

This chart removes the COVID years to reveal the normal seasonal pattern of tourism demand before the disruption.

Why it matters: It shows which months were typically stronger before the shock.

Where tourists go

Destination performance across Malaysian states

International Hotel Guests by State Map

This map shows where international hotel demand was strongest in the latest complete state-level year available.

Why it matters: Geography reveals which states concentrated tourism demand.

Hotel Occupancy Rate by State

This chart shows hotel utilisation, which is different from guest volume. A state can attract fewer guests but still use rooms efficiently.

Why it matters: Occupancy shows efficiency, not only popularity.

Guest Volume vs Occupancy Rate

This chart separates high-volume states from high-efficiency states. The upper-right area represents stronger performance on both measures.

Why it matters: States in the upper-right combine demand and strong hotel utilisation.
